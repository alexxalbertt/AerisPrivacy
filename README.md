# 🔐 Privacy Policy — AerisCrew / FatigueScope

*Last updated: March 2026*

---

## 1. Overview

AerisCrew (also referred to as "FatigueScope") is a professional aviation awareness application designed for flight crew.

This application is:

* Informational only
* Not a medical tool
* Not a legal or regulatory system
* Not connected to any airline systems

Your data is **yours**, and the app is designed with a **privacy-first, local-first approach**.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 User-Provided Data

The app may store:

* Duty-related inputs (dates, times, routes)
* Sleep and fatigue-related inputs
* Operational context (delays, workload, etc.)
* Notes entered by the user

This data is used **only to provide fatigue awareness within the app**.

---

### 2.2 Automatically Processed Data

If enabled, the app may process:

* Basic authentication data (Google Sign-In)
* Device-local timestamps

No sensitive biometric or health data is collected unless explicitly implemented in future versions.

---

## 3. Data Storage

### 🔒 Local-First Principle

* All data is stored **locally on your device by default**
* No cloud storage is required
* No automatic data sharing

---

### ☁️ Backend (if applicable)

If backend services are used:

* Data is stored securely
* Only minimal required data is transmitted
* No sharing with third parties

---

## 4. Authentication (Google Sign-In)

If you choose to sign in with Google:

* We only access basic profile information (e.g. email)
* Authentication is handled securely via Google OAuth
* No access to your Google data beyond authentication

---

## 5. Data Sharing

We do **NOT**:

* Share your data with airlines
* Share your data with third parties
* Sell your data
* Use your data for advertising

---

## 6. Data Ownership

You retain full ownership of your data.

You can:

* Delete the app → all local data is removed
* Clear storage → data is permanently deleted

---

## 7. Security

We implement:

* Secure authentication flows
* Minimal data collection
* Local-first architecture

However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

---

## 8. Intended Use

This app is intended for:

* Airline pilots
* Cabin crew

It is **not intended for general public use**.

---

## 9. Disclaimer

AerisCrew / FatigueScope:

* Does NOT determine fitness to fly
* Does NOT replace regulatory frameworks (e.g. EASA)
* Does NOT provide medical advice
* Does NOT make operational decisions

All outputs are **informational only**.
